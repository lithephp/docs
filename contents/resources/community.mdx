## Join Our Discord!

Connect with Lithe developers and enthusiasts! In our Discord channel, you will find a friendly and welcoming environment where you can share ideas, ask questions, and collaborate on innovative projects.

<a href="https://discord.gg/nfskM6x9x7" target="_blank"><strong>Join the Discord</strong></a>

**What you can find on Discord:**

- **Discussions on New Features:** Stay updated with the latest news and contribute your ideas and feedback.
- **Support for Bugs and Issues:** Get help from other community members to resolve technical issues and improve your experience with Lithe.
- **Contributions to Open-Source Projects:** Join us in developing Lithe and help make it even better with your contributions.
- **Tips and Best Development Practices:** Learn from the experience of other developers and improve your skills.
- **Networking:** Connect with other professionals and make new friendships that can last a lifetime!

## How to Contribute

If you want to contribute to Lithe, there are several ways to do so:

- **Report Bugs:** Found a bug? Don’t hesitate to report it in our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/lithephp/).
- **Contribute Code:** Feel free to submit pull requests with improvements, fixes, or new features.
- **Create Content:** Help enrich our knowledge base by creating tutorials or articles that can benefit other users.
- **Share Projects:** If you’ve developed something using Lithe, share it with the community and inspire other developers!
